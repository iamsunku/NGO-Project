Frequently asked questions

01. What are some fun and effective ways to teach basic shapes and colours for young minds?

Use games such as colour sorting games, shape related games like moulding clay into different shapes. Provide puzzle, building blocks

02. How can I use technology in teaching math?

Use interactive software and applications that make math learning engaging and interactive. Incorporate multimedia resources and online tools to facilitate better understanding.

02. How can I use technology in teaching math?

Use educational apps like do your math, Photo math, Symbolab

03. How can I support a child/ student with a learning disability in math?

Engage multiple senses during instruction Use manipulatives like blocks or counters, draw diagrams, and incorporate movement activities to reinforce concepts

08. What are some effective ways to teach grammar?

Use games, activities, and technology to make learning grammar more engaging.

09. How can I use technology to enhance language learning?

Use apps like Duolingo, Memrise, or Babbel for bite-sized lessons, vocabulary games, and personalized learning paths
