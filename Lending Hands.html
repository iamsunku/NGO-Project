Our Aspiration

"Lending a Hand" is an online meeting place where people seeking help in the areas of improving competency in teaching or caregiving and people who can help the people in need. Our goal is to facilitate this exchange of support and lending hand with a degree of curation but without any intervention or intermediation fees.
